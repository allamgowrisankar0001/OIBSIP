Fitting Tips

  • Measure your foot in the evening when it's at its largest
  • Wear the same type of socks you'll wear with the shoes
  • Leave about 1cm of space at the toe for comfort
  • Walk around to ensure the heel doesn't slip
  • If between sizes, choose the larger size
